⬆️ Upgrade Service Details
  • Basic subscribers see "Upgrade to Comprehensive" on their subscriptions page
  • One-time upgrade fee: $300 (VAT inclusive — shown as $258.62 + $41.38 VAT)
  • After upgrade, renewal follows standard Comprehensive rate: $100/month
  • Payment via bank transfer or PayPal (same as regular orders)
  • Admin approves and processes upgrade after payment confirmation
💵 VAT-Inclusive Pricing All listed prices already include 16% VAT. The checkout displays the price breakdown (e.g., $500 package shown as $431.03 + $68.97 VAT = $500 total). The customer always pays the listed price — no extra charges.

 🔴 High Revenue ~2.9% per transaction
